In this video, I am giving a lesson based on conversations from two different styles of Izakaya I actually visited. I also explain how to order drinks, the rules for "All-you-can-drink" (Nomihodai), and the "Otoshi" (appetizer) charged as a seating fee.
I hope you can learn and enjoy not only the Japanese language but also Japan's Izakaya culture through this video. Please practice listening and speaking multiple times through shadowing and role-playing, and enjoy going to an Izakaya in Japan!
